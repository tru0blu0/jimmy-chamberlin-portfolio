To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to provide my highest recommendation for Jimmy Chamberlin, who served as Logistics Operations Manager for J&J Freight Logistics, LLC (a subsidiary of J&J Auto Express, LLC) from February 2017 through March 2020. Jimmy reported directly to me during this period and was responsible for building and operating our freight brokerage division from the ground up.

When Jimmy started, we had a fleet of trucks and an idea — but no brokerage infrastructure whatsoever. We had no FMCSA broker authority, no transportation management system, no carrier network, and our drivers were still on paper logs with a federal ELD mandate six months away. Jimmy built the entire operational foundation.

"Jimmy built our freight brokerage operation from absolute zero. He secured our FMCSA authority, selected and implemented our TMS, got every truck ELD-compliant ahead of the federal deadline, and built the carrier onboarding and compliance systems from scratch. In three years under his operational management, we had zero FMCSA enforcement actions and qualified as an SDDC DoD Certified Carrier. He didn't just do the work — he documented it so thoroughly that his successor could step in without missing a beat."

What distinguished Jimmy was his ability to operate independently. He didn't wait to be told what needed doing — he identified gaps, researched solutions, and executed. The VehicHaul TMS migration he led in 2019 eliminated a double-entry bottleneck that had been frustrating our team for over a year. He trained himself on the platform, configured the workflows, and documented every step.

Jimmy's operational discipline was exceptional. He maintained meticulous carrier records, enforced ePOD compliance across our network, and built reconciliation processes that caught billing discrepancies before they became disputes. His systems-thinking approach meant that every process he designed included the documentation, training materials, and compliance checks needed to sustain it without him.

I would not hesitate to hire Jimmy again or recommend him for any operations leadership role. He brings a rare combination of strategic vision and hands-on execution — he can design the system and then build it himself. Any organization would be stronger with him on the team.
