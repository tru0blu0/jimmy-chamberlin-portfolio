To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to provide my strongest recommendation for Jimmy Chamberlin, who served as Operations Manager at The Pointe Bar & Grill from May 2014 through September 2016. Jimmy was hired into a struggling operation and within two years had transformed it into the highest-performing location in our portfolio.

When Jimmy took over, The Pointe was doing roughly $734,000 in annual revenue with no financial controls in place. There was no P&L system, no documented processes, no vendor management strategy, and no staffing model across 29 employees and 7 roles. Alcohol costs were running unchecked at 36–42%. We were flying blind.

"Jimmy took a restaurant doing $734K and turned it into a $1.25M operation. He built the P&L system from scratch — a 19-sheet Excel workbook that gave me visibility into every cost center I'd never been able to see before. He got our costs under control, implemented a dual-vendor strategy that improved our margins, and documented every process so the restaurant ran whether he was there or not. In 30 years in this business, I've never seen someone build operational infrastructure that thoroughly. Best operational hire I ever made."

What set Jimmy apart was his business-first mindset. He didn't just manage the restaurant — he managed the financial performance. He built a 19-sheet Excel P&L workbook that tracked revenue, food costs, alcohol costs, and labor across 76 columns of daily data. For the first time, I could see exactly where every dollar was going. He held alcohol COGS at 31.7% for 89 consecutive weeks — a level of cost discipline our other locations never achieved.

Jimmy also managed our vendor relationships strategically. He brought in Sysco as a second distributor alongside GFS, creating competition that improved pricing and service levels across both vendors. He tracked 2,561 invoices across 203 vendors in a 30-category ledger. When he left, he handed me a complete operations manual with SOPs for every role in the building.

Jimmy is the kind of operator who makes ownership's life easier, not harder. He brings data to decisions, builds systems that outlast him, and treats the business's money like his own. I recommend him without any reservation.

Sincerely,
